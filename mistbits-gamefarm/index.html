How It Works

Deployed in Days, Not Months

No trenching. No expensive cabling. Our mesh loggers talk to each other over LoRa radio ‚Äî covering vast distances without cellular infrastructure.

1

Site Survey

We map your property, identify monitoring points, and design the optimal mesh network topology.

2

Deploy Nodes

Rugged mesh loggers are installed at each monitoring point ‚Äî boreholes, fences, solar arrays, dams.

3

Mesh Connects

Nodes form a self-healing LoRa mesh network. A single gateway bridges data to the cloud via 2G/4G or WiFi.

4

Monitor & Control

Access your dashboard from anywhere. Real-time data, alerts, remote control ‚Äî all from your phone or PC.
